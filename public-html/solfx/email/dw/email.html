Drivewear Transitions SOLFX lenese are designed specifically to provide the ideal visual performance solutions for drivers, by changing and optimizing their degree of darkness and color with the sun. The only polarized photochromic to activate behind the windshield of a car, these lenses combine NuPolar® polarization and advanced Transitions photochromic dyes, which respond to both visible light and UV light.

The many benefits of Drivewear lenses include:

  • Enhanced visual performance (like traffic signal recognition and depth perception)
  • Reduced eyestrain and fatigue from glare and bright sunlight
  • Blocks 100% UVA and UVB rays
  • Style that keeps you looking great on the road
